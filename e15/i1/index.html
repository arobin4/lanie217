Instructions for picture

  • Create a 8.5 by 11 inch document in Illustrator
  • This picture will have no color, so it will just be black and white
  • the overall shape should be drawn in the center slightly on the left side and slighty towards the top
  • Go to the curvature pen tool and create a oval-circle shape as you see from the reference
  • Once you have the basic shape, you are going to use the curvature tool again to create a curved line inside of the shape you just made.
  • Once you have made that curved line then you are going to duplicate that line 2 times
  • You are then going to use the curvature tool again to create a curved line on the opposite end inside the shape
  • You will then duplicate that curved line 1 time
  • There should be space between the lines inside the shape, be aware of that
